About Me

I am a Systems Designer currently studying Digital Media with a specialization in Game Design at the University of Central Florida. While I specialize in systems design, I also have experience working with many areas of game programming including AI behaviour, combat systems, and movement systems.

I enjoy designing and implementing interconnected gameplay systems that feel responsive, readable, and satisfying for players.
